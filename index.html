PowerShell Conference Europe 2019


About

PowerShell Conference Europe is the largest PowerShell-themed annual conference in Europe. Our goal for psconf.eu 2019 is a joyful “Recalibration Week” where we put together all the opportunities that PowerShell offers in a modern world, and make this week a learning experience for everyone.

If you haven’t attended psconf.eu before, expect a friendly and passionate audience of 350 professionals and 40 international top speakers. You can build your personal agenda and pick the topics that matter the most to you by using our conference app (iOS, Android), and choose from three parallel tracks. The agenda and abstracts will be published by end of January 2019.

Most sessions are held in English, a few in German. Conference languages are English, German, and HandAndFeet.

So why do people from 35 countries travel to Hannover and attend this conference?

  • Unparalleled content: our approximately 80 sessions cover in depth the topics requested most by advanced PowerShell and automation experts: we look at little-known language features and optimizations that make a difference in the real world, present industry best- practices and experiences that can save weeks of time, provide authoritative guidance on new technology so you can update your knowhow and invest your resources wisely. Topics include (but are by far not limited to) Windows PowerShell, PowerShell Core, Database, Remoting Techniques, Cross Platform Management, Security, Cloud Services, IoT, Virtualization, DSC, Code Optimization, Graphical User Interfaces, and Tools.
  • Out of the world speakers: With a ratio of 1 speaker per 10 delegates, not only has psconf.eu a very personal touch. The speakers are renown international industry experts, and you probably already know some of their work. Many of them have created widely used PowerShell extensions or have written books. Some authored PowerShell itself, like keynote speaker and PowerShell inventor Jeffrey Snover or the engineers from the Microsoft PowerShell team who work on PowerShell Core and DSC. Others authored essential PowerShell tools and extensions: You’ll meet Amazon engineers working on the AWS PowerShell module. You’ll meet some of the bright minds behind dbatools, the extension for database admins. You not just hear about Pester (for unit testing), you can talk to their maintainers. The same is true for ISESteroids (for advanced editing in ISE), Empire (for penetration testing), and many more. Other speakers have authored books like the famous “PowerShell Cookbook”, or the German “Windows PowerShell 5 Handbuch”. And they are all looking forward to meeting you and exchanging thoughts and ideas. You return with the most authoritative and first-hand answer to your questions – plus a lot of new contacts.
  • Out of the world delegates: while you certainly don’t have to be a PowerShell wizard to attend, a lot of wizards do. It’s the talks in between sessions that are magic moments during the event. You can just silently suck in information, that’s perfectly ok. You’ll quickly find yourself in a warm and friendly environment, though, that is curious to hear back from you. So bring your questions, share your findings and maybe a chilled beer or two at the Zoo evening event. If you dare, during lunch time the stage is yours, and in 15 and 30 minute “Birds of Feather” sessions you can test drive your career as a future speaker, present tricks and techniques, and discuss with delegates and speakers.

psconf.eu often feels more like a 4-day training event for the seasoned PowerShell professional. Which coincidentally is why you can choose at the end of the registration form whether you would like to be invoiced for a conference admission or an advanced PowerShell training event – however you like to call it, wherever your budget comes from, in the true essence there is no richer and more versatile 4-day advanced PowerShell training imaginable.

That’s because our agenda and our sessions are essentially a stream of brain teasers. What’s important are the people in the room, the questions they ask, the discussions raised, and the learning points that follow. For this reason, psconf.eu features a zillion of short coffee breaks following almost every session, and the famous Zoo evening event on day 1 with country reception provides all the opportunity to get yourself connected with others quickly.

Admission includes all coffee breaks, snacks, lunch, and the Zoo evening event. Travel and accommodation is not included. The conference is organized by the community, for the community! We are happy to welcome you to yet another brilliant conference on June 4-7, 2019. Will we see you there?
