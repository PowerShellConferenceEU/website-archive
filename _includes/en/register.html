Normal registration fee is EUR 999, but for the first 100 registrations, we lower the fee to EUR 899,- so it pays to register early!

Registration is limited to 160 attendees on a first come, first serve basis.

Registration fee is EUR 899,- (including 19% VAT) for the first 100 registrations and EUR 999,- after that, and covers all three conference days. It also includes food and beverages throughout the conference days, and the evening event including food and drinks.

The price does not include accomodation. We have reserved a hotel contingent for you (100 rooms in the congress hotel right next to the venue). You can also search for accommodation in Hannover yourself. Make sure it is located close to a tram station.

When you register, we send an invoice to the email address you specify, with the billing address you specified. You then have 30 days to pay the invoice, provided there are still seats available. After 30 days, your registration request is automatically cancelled.

Once the invoice is paid, you receive a registration confirmation. If you decide to not pay the invoice in time, your registration is automatically cancelled. If you pay the invoice after all seats were already taken, we reimburse your payment. If you need changes to your invoice before you paid (address change etc), register again with the correct details.

If you need changes after you registered (i.e. attendee changes), or if you have other questions, don't hesitate to contact us.
