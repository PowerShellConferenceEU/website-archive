About

In 2016, psconf.eu was the largest PowerShell conference world-wide, with 80+ sessions and 45 speakers from 5 continents.

In 2017, we again take a close look at all the exciting new extensions to the PowerShell ecosystem (i.e. PowerShell on Linux, Server 2016, Nanoserver on Azure, etc.), we’ll cover the opportunities introduced by Microsoft’s new “Open Source” strategy, we’ll look at hot topics like security, SQL Server tuning, real-world best practices, discuss how code can be maintained and distributed within your company teams or publicly, and leave plenty of room to discuss, learn, and look ahead.


We target Administrators, DevOps, Consultants, and technical decision makers.
